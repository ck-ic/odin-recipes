Spicy Habanero Chili

Chili

This spicy dish of chili will heat you up on a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs sirloin steak cut up 1/2 cubes
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 green pepper chopped
  • 3 to 5 jalape√±os chopped
  • 1 to 2 habanero chopped
  • 1 poblano pepper chopped
  • 1 to 2 habanero chopped
  • 1 medium sweet onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup beer
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 15oz cans tomato sauce
  • 2 15oz cans pinto beans
  • 2 15oz cans black beans
  • 2 15oz cans kidney beans

Steps

  1. Brown cubed sirloin in a 4 quart pot.
  2. Stir in chopped peppers onion and garlic.
  3. Simmer stirring occasionally for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Add beer, stir in chili powder, black pepper, cumin, and brown sugar.
  5. Bring back up to simmer stirring occasionally for 5 to 10 minutes.
  6. Add tomato sauce, and drained - rinsed beans.
  7. Bring back up to Simmer stirring occasionally for approximately 30 minutes.
  8. Optional - Serve with shredded cheese, sour cream, and fresh chopped cilantro as toppings.
