Mum's Spicy Rigatoni dish

Rigatoni

This spicy cheesy dish of rigatoni will heat you up on a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds hamburger
  • 1 67 ounce ragu traditional spaghetti sauce.
  • 1 pound box rigatoni noodles
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown and drain hamburger in a 4 quart pot.
  2. Stir in ragu sauce, sugar, cayenne pepper.
  3. Simmer low stirring occasionally for 30 minutes.
  4. Boil and drain rigatoni noodles.
  5. Add rigatoni noodles into the sauce.
  6. Stir well.
  7. Add mozzarella cheese and stir well.
  8. Serve.
